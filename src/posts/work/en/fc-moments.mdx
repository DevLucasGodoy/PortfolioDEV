## Overview

**FC Moments** is a SaaS platform for managing events and special moments. From birthdays to get-togethers and corporate events, it centralizes guests, tasks, budget and vendors on a single screen.

_"Every moment, in the right place."_

## Problem

Organizing an event becomes a puzzle of WhatsApp groups, spreadsheets and loose notes. Guests who don't confirm, forgotten tasks and a budget that blows up without anyone noticing.

## Solution

Everything an event needs, organized:

- **Guests** with RSVP and real-time headcount
- **Tasks** with deadlines, owners and checklist
- **Budget** with spend tracking by category
- **Vendors** with contacts and contracting status
- **Timeline** of the event, from planning to the day

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS (responsive)
- **Backend**: Node.js + REST API
- **Database**: PostgreSQL with per-event access control
- **Infra**: cloud deploy

## Impact

FC Moments takes the event out of the mess of spreadsheets and messages and puts everything in one place. Less forgetting, more control — and more time to enjoy the moment.
