## Overview

**Servix** is a SaaS platform for work-order management, built for field service teams and repair shops still tracking jobs on paper. It centralizes tickets, technicians, customers and history in one place.

_"Less paper. More service."_

## Problem

Technical service companies lose time and money with paper work orders: lost tickets, status nobody knows, rework and no customer history. The manager has no real-time view of operations.

## Solution

A full digital flow from opening to closing the work order:

- **Work orders** with status, priority, assignee and checklist
- **Schedule** of technicians with job distribution
- **Customers** with full equipment and service history
- **Mobile-friendly** for technicians to update orders in the field
- **Reports** on productivity, average resolution time and SLA

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS (responsive / PWA)
- **Backend**: Node.js + REST API
- **Database**: PostgreSQL with role-based access control
- **Infra**: cloud deploy

## Impact

Servix kills the paper work order and gives managers real-time visibility into operations. Less rework, faster service, and a history that turns into business intelligence.
