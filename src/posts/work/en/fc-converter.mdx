## Overview

**FC Converter** converts images, audio, video and documents **100% in the browser**. No upload, no server, no account, no limits and nothing to pay — all processing runs on the user's device with WebAssembly.

_"Convert any file, free and unlimited — without anything leaving your device."_

## Problem

Most online converters send your file to a server, convert it there and send it back. That means upload waits, plan-based limits, watermarks — and real doubt about what happens to contracts, medical documents and personal photos after upload.

## Solution

### Privacy by architecture

FC Converter has **no conversion server**: nothing is sent, nothing is stored, nothing is accessed. It's not a privacy-policy promise — it's the product's architecture. We can't see what we never receive.

### 200+ supported formats

- **Images**: PNG, JPG, WEBP, GIF, BMP, TIFF and more — including ICO (favicon) and SVG
- **Audio**: MP3, WAV, OGG, AAC, FLAC, M4A and more
- **Video**: MP4, WEBM, MOV, AVI, MKV — including audio extraction from video
- **Documents**: DOCX, PDF, TXT, HTML, EPUB, XLSX and CSV, with cross conversions (DOCX → PDF, PDF → DOCX/PNG, XLSX ↔ CSV and more)

### Actually free, actually unlimited

- **$0, forever** — no paid plan, no watermark, no 7-day trial
- **No file limit**: convert 1 or 100 at once, with batch drag and drop
- **No account**: no sign-up, no email, no password
- Engines loaded **on demand**: the site opens light and only downloads the engine for the file type you use
- Bilingual (PT/EN), light/dark/auto theme and opt-in analytics (off by default)

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js 16 (App Router), React 19, TypeScript
- **Media**: FFmpeg compiled to WebAssembly — the same industry-standard engine, running in the browser
- **Documents**: pdf-lib, pdf.js, mammoth, docx, exceljs and jszip, loaded on demand
- **Special images**: Canvas API for ICO and SVG
- **Architecture**: 100% client-side — no conversion API route exists in the product

## Impact

FC Converter removes the trade-off between convenience and privacy: instant conversion, no upload queue, on any device with a modern browser. The converter where the file never travels — useful for everyone from content creators to offices handling confidential material.
