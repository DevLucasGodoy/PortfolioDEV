## Overview

**Velaris CRM** is a web application for business management, built for digital agencies, consultancies and technology companies that want to centralize operations and scale intelligently.

Instead of fragmented tools, Velaris brings everything a company needs to operate into a fluid, responsive interface: projects, finance, clients, support and AI automation.

## Problem

Agencies and technology companies often operate with a scattered set of tools — one for projects, another for finance, another for CRM. This creates rework, lack of visibility and decisions made on stale data.

## Solution

An integrated platform with all the essential management modules in a single place:

- **Dashboard** with real-time KPIs and alerts
- **Projects** in Kanban, Gantt and list views with deadline control and collaboration
- **Finance** with invoices, cash flow and automated income statements
- **Clients** with pipeline, history and segmentation
- **Tickets** with automatic SLA and prioritization
- **Reports** with AI-generated insights

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React 18, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion
- **Backend**: Supabase (PostgreSQL, Auth, Edge Functions)
- **Security**: Row Level Security (RLS), LGPD compliance
- **Infra**: Vite 5, deployed via Vercel

## Impact

The platform removed the need for multiple tools in the day-to-day of agencies, centralizing operations and reducing the time spent on manual reports. Real-time visibility into projects and finances enabled faster, data-driven decisions.
