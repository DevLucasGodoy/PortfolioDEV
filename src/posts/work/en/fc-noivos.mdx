## Overview

**FC Noivos** is a complete wedding planning platform. It combines the public site guests visit with an admin dashboard where the couple centralizes every decision — guest list, budget, day-of timeline, gifts and much more — under a single visual identity.

_"A beautiful site for the guests and a complete dashboard for the couple, with built-in Pix."_

## Problem

Planning a wedding means managing dozens of fronts at once — guests, tables, vendors, budget, deadlines. It usually lives scattered across spreadsheets, chats and third-party gift registries, and any forgotten detail is costly.

## Solution

### For the guests (public site)

- **Home** with full-screen cover photo, countdown and shortcuts to RSVP and View the Venue
- **Venue** with embedded Google Map, ceremony and reception times and a photo gallery of the space
- **Accommodation** (optional) with a curated hotel list — perfect for out-of-town relatives
- **Gift Registry** with group gifts, valid Pix QR Code (Bacen-standard BR Code) or automated charging via Abacate Pay
- **RSVP wizard**: lookup by name or invitation PIN, individual attendance per group member and kid-specific questions
- **Guestbook** with public messages on a masonry wall
- **Custom identity**: fully editable color palette, photos, copy and couple's monogram

### For the couple (admin dashboard)

- **Guests** with filters, grouping, RSVP status by origin and drag-and-drop seating plan
- **Wedding party** management with role, side, responsibilities and a color palette per member
- **Tasks** in kanban, unified monthly **calendar** and hour-by-hour **day-of timeline**, exportable for vendors
- **Budget** with categories, installments with down payment, per-installment due dates and a dedicated Installments & Due Dates panel
- **Gifts** with optional Abacate Pay integration to receive Pix automatically
- **Ideas** as a Pinterest-style mood board, with boards per category and PDF export
- **Honeymoon** planning with its own budget, notification center with optional emails, team with three roles (Groom, Bride, Wedding Planner) and appearance with five configurable colors and live preview

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js 16 (App Router), React 19, TypeScript and Tailwind CSS 4 with custom tokens that respond to the couple's configured theme
- **Database**: serverless PostgreSQL 16 (Neon), accessed via `@neondatabase/serverless` with parameterized queries
- **Payments**: native Abacate Pay (Pix) integration, or manual Pix via key + QR Code
- **Uploads**: images on Cloudflare R2 with real content validation (magic bytes); financial receipts in a private bucket served only through an authenticated route
- **Auth**: signed httpOnly session cookie (HMAC), scrypt + salt password hashing, rate limiting and origin validation

## Impact

FC Noivos turns planning chaos into a clear, calm dashboard. No loose spreadsheets, no gift registry on a third-party vendor: the couple tracks everything in one place, receives Pix gifts straight into their account and reaches the big day feeling in control — not in a rush.
