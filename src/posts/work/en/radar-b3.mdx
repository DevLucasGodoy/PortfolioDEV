## Overview

**Radar B3** is a SaaS stock market analysis platform built for investors who want to decide with data, not guesswork. It tracks B3 assets and delivers real-time signals, dashboards and alerts in a single interface.

_"Where strategy meets technology."_

## Problem

Retail investors drown in scattered information — quotes in one place, indicators in another, news in a third. Without a consolidated view, opportunities slip away and decisions arrive late.

## Solution

A dashboard that centralizes what matters to trade:

- **Dashboards** with quotes, variation and indicators for tracked assets
- **Signals** generated from technical rules and fundamentals
- **Alerts** in real time by price, variation and market events
- **Watchlists** tailored to profile and strategy
- **History** to review decisions and refine your own market reading

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend**: Node.js, integration with market quote APIs
- **Data**: PostgreSQL, collection and time-series processing jobs
- **Infra**: cloud deploy with queues for asynchronous alerts

## Impact

Radar B3 shortens the gap between "seeing the market" and "making the call", putting signals and alerts in the same place. Instead of juggling tabs and spreadsheets, the investor opens one dashboard and acts.
